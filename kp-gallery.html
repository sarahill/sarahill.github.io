Kapost Gallery App

UX Design Lead, Visual Design Lead, Front-End Development

Product Overview

Kapost Gallery streamlines distribution of your files both internally and externally. These assets can be distributed to anyone that needs to access to information quickly. Create Collections, add content and share your files with anyone -- even if they don't have a Kapost account.

Design System

Designing a visual system for a product this large was an incredible experience! Over the course of 2 quick months working closely with my teammates I created everything you see on this page. Kapost UX is a very small and skilled UX team of 3 people. We work very quickly and in close proximity with our Developers and Product Owners.

Admin All Content View

Allows users to view, edit, and upload all of their content in one place. Users can add new content that will sync to the Kapost Factory and add content collections to easily share out assets to anyone.

Admin Consumer Preview

Consumer Preview allows users to get full insight into what visitors to Shared Collections will see.
